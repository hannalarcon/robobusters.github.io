1/6/18

It is finally time for another season of FRC. This year, it is FIRST Powerup. To reveal this year's challenge, we will have a kickoff from the North Texas Region. At the Kick off we will see the entire game field, be given the game rules, basic training, and have a game break down with some other mentors. We recommend all students who can attend do attend. All we know now is that the game is called FIRST Power UP.

The 2018 FRC Season starts Saturday 01/06/2018, at the TI South Campus (Texas Instruments 12500 TI Blvd Dallas, TX 75243). We will be meeting at Townview in the morning and going on a bus to the TI campus. We will leave at 11 from the campus back to Townview. YOU MUST BE GOING ON THE BUS. You are not allowed to travel on your own to the campus.

For veterans: if you have a Robobusters shirt, please wear it.

Here is the schedle for the day:

  • 8:00a Doors Open / Check In Begins / Light Breakfast / Early Kit Distribution (as appropriate)
  • 9:00a Welcome to TI
  • 9:30a FIRST 2018 Kickoff
  • 10:00a Field Viewing
  • 10:30a Live Game Break Down
  • 11:00a Return to Townview
